What is Wordpress?

WordPress is one of the most popular and actual content and blogs publisher on the internet, it was release in 2003 as a blog service provider and nowadays it expanded its boundaries becoming a full feature and full customizable tool for web development.

The WordPress community its large and spread all over the world, so its very easy to find solutions for many problems you face on your development on its forums, since open source material has plenty collaborators you will be able to manage your websites by yourself.

WordPress is a free open source set of tolls for commercial and private use, that can be installed in your computer and customized as you will.

There is no complications towards create your website , its simple as edit a text in any text editor.

Its been evolving since its creation and its able to create any type of website and even power mobile applications.

All websites need to have a place where will store its contents and that can be accessed by anywhere in the world, Wordpress.com has its own host service that can be used to store your projects.

Once you upload your website you receive a address that can be shared to the world. Normally the address is associated to your domain name for example “www.yourdomainname.com”

Once you project is stored it will receive a web address that you can share and it will lead people to your content, that address is called IP-Address.

ts a set of number with dot like this (192.168.255.1) or physical address for your website /content of your domain.

Wich is the rigth one for you?

The difference between WordPress.com and WordPress.org is who's actually hosting your website.

Where wordpress.com provided the full service with hosting and WordPress.org, youhave to host your own blog or website.

At WordPress.org is where you'll find the free WordPress software that you can download and install on your own web server.

“For those who choose to use Wordpress.org there is only one administrator interface. But for you who choose Wordpress.com there are two. ”

Backend / Frontend!

Frontend its most associated with how the website will look and will be displayed to the user, basically the beauty display, buttons,menus, images ,animations in some cases dealing with user input and final appearance.

Behind the scene we have all coding and algorithms to make those animations work properly, the forms linked to the proper database, the connection between pages and the safety to access the page rather need a account and password (membership) all this part that deal with all administrative and functionally of a website/server is the backend .
